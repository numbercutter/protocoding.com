# Jordan Writing Sample: AI & Technology Perspective

## Sample Info
- **Topic**: AI capabilities, data privacy, technology trajectory
- **Context**: Transcribed discussion / interview
- **Length**: ~300 words

---

## Voice Characteristics Demonstrated

- Technical confidence without arrogance
- Pragmatic/realistic about technology capabilities
- Ethical awareness (data privacy considerations)
- Direct communication style
- Balances optimism with practical constraints

---

## Sample Content

I don't, as of right now, think there's any world where we fail in this or find a brick wall. There's a lot of technology, especially black technology—by black technology, I mean stuff the military industrial complex is developing, things from DARPA, which is basically the deepest, darkest technology our government has. That's where a lot of innovation starts from. I think especially with energy, we're not going to face a barrier at all.

But essentially what this company does is we're taking the work out of evaluating homes by applying AI. We're evolving the real estate appraisal world by automating appraisal reports. It takes about two hours on average to fill out an appraisal report.

On the data question—I think there's a good and a bad part. The good part is we're able to make a much better product if we know exactly what data we're working with. But there's also the privacy problem. I think if you can define a system where the user agrees consensually that they're willing to share their data, then there should be no problem with that.

In my experience, if I could talk to the viewers here: don't go out stealing data. That's all I can say. I don't know what other companies are doing. If they are, I'd tell them to stop. I wouldn't tell them to operate in such a way because I think that's just bad faith with their users. You're not going to be a successful company if you do something like that.

---

## Notable Patterns for Article Writing

- **Technical grounding**: References specific tech (DARPA, energy tech)
- **Problem framing**: Identifies pain points clearly (2 hours for appraisal)
- **Ethical stance**: Takes clear positions on data privacy
- **Direct address**: "If I could talk to the viewers here"
- **Practical optimism**: Confident about possibilities, realistic about constraints
